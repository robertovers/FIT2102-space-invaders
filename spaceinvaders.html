space invaders

by Robert Overs

Controls:
Move player by moving mouse across the screen or using left and right arrow keys.
Shoot by clicking whilst on screen or by pressing the 'x' key.

Gameplay:
You get 10 points for each Alien shot.
When you shoot all aliens on the screen, you will progress to the next level.
The game ends if you are shot or an alien reaches the bottom.
If the game ends, click the screen to play again.
